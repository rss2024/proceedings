Robotics: Science and Systems II

Pursuit and evasion in non-convex domains of arbitrary dimensions

S. Alexander, R. Bishop, R. Ghrist

Abstract: Most results in pursuit-evasion games apply only to planar domains or perhaps to higher-dimensional domains which must be convex. We introduce a very general set of techniques to generalize and extend certain results on simple pursuit to non-convex domains of arbitrary dimension which satisfy a coarse curvature condition (the CAT(0) condition).
